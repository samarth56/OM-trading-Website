About Us

Om Trading Corporation, founded in 2020, began with a focus on corn farming and quickly expanded into the export of corn mill products. Over time, the company diversified into importing and exporting raw materials, products, and equipment, supporting industries and affiliated joint ventures. As part of our growth strategy, we became distributors for several overseas companies, establishing a strong presence in Malaysia, Nepal, Bhutan, and Bangladesh. This expansion has allowed us to remain competitive and broaden our reach in the global marketplace. Our commitment to excellence and innovation drives us to deliver high-quality solutions, creating long-term value for our clients and partners across industries.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster sustainable global trade and provide innovative solutions that bridge industries with efficient logistics and high-quality resources.

Our Vision

To be the world's leading trading company, empowering businesses with exceptional service, integrity, and innovation. We aim to redefine the future of global trade by continually adapting to new market dynamics and technological advancements.
